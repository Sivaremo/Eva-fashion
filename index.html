Our Story

Defining the Art of Fashion for a Generation

Founded in Paris in 2009, Eva Fashion was born from a singular vision: to create a modeling agency that transcended trends and celebrated the timeless art of fashion storytelling.

With a portfolio spanning over 320 international shows across six continents, we have built a reputation as the premier destination for brands seeking models who embody both beauty and character.

Our curated roster of talent — carefully selected for their unique presence, professional dedication, and versatility — has graced the runways of Paris, Milan, New York, Tokyo, and London.

"Fashion is not about clothes. It is about confidence, identity, and the story you choose to tell the world."

— Éva Laurent, Founder & Creative Director
